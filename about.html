About Us

Get to know the Synthwave Summit experience!

Our Mission

Synthwave Summit is the ultimate celebration of retro-futuristic music, bringing together enthusiasts from all over the world to experience an unforgettable weekend of immersive sounds, amazing performances, and a true sense of community. Our mission is to showcase the best of synthwave and electronic music while embracing the nostalgic energy of the 80s and 90s.

Our History

Founded in 2020, Synthwave Summit started as a small underground gathering for music lovers and has since grown into one of the most anticipated events for synthwave and electronic music fans. We have been lucky to partner with some of the most innovative artists in the genre and are excited to continue creating unforgettable experiences for all our attendees.
