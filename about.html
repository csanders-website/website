About Us

Get to know the Synthwave Summit experience!

Our Mission

Synthwave Summit is a celebration of the retro-futuristic music, bringing people together from all over the world to enjoy a weekend filled with incredible musicians, incredible performances, and a strong community. Our desire was to bring together the best of synthwave and electronic music.

Our History

Starting 2025, Synthwave Summit will be a large gathering for music lovers and will grow into one of the most anticipated events for synthwave and electronic music fans. We have been lucky to get some of the most innovative artists in the genre to perform and are excited to create an unforgettable experiences for all our attendees.
